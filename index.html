Spend most of your time away from the main campus?
We’ve got you covered. Discovery Perks Market and Grill,
across from the library at Disco Park, serves up hot,
fresh, made-to-order entrées daily, as well as Starbucks — so
there’s no shortage of delicious options, whatever you crave.



It’s a great place to eat delicious meals and connect with friends.
Enjoy our custom salad station or hop over to the Grill for made-
to-order favorites and hearty home-style meals.

Enjoy Starbucks drinks, Clark Bakery treats, Verde Everyday
grab-and-go products, Scrappy’s Ice Cream, and much more.
Fuel up on your way to class!
